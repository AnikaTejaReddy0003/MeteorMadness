Meteor Madness

About the Application

Meteor Madness is a comprehensive web application that brings the wonders of space exploration to your fingertips. Powered by NASA's official APIs, this platform provides real-time data about Near-Earth Objects (NEOs) and showcases stunning astronomical imagery. Whether you're a space enthusiast, researcher, or simply curious about celestial phenomena, Meteor Madness offers an intuitive interface to explore the cosmos and track potentially hazardous asteroids approaching Earth.
